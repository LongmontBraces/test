About Longmont Braces

At Longmont Braces, board-certified orthodontists Dr. Stephanie Ross and Dr. Aden Peterson are passionate about creating healthy, confident smiles that last a lifetime. We’re a warm, fun, and patient-focused practice where everyone is treated like family—not a number.

Whether you’re looking for traditional braces, clear braces, Invisalign®, or ClearCorrect® clear aligners, we offer the latest options customized to fit your lifestyle and budget. Ready for a smile you’ll love? Visit longmontbraces.com to schedule your complimentary consultation today!

Our Treatment Philosophy

We believe every smile tells a story. From teen orthodontics and adult orthodontics to Invisalign® and Invisalign Teen®, plus early intervention for children, we tailor treatment plans to fit your lifestyle, goals, and budget. Our focus is not just straight teeth — it’s lifelong oral health, confidence, and comfort throughout your journey.

Why Patients Choose Us

With state-of-the-art digital scanning, flexible payment plans, and a team certified in the latest orthodontic techniques, Longmont Braces stands out as a trusted partner in your smile transformation. We’re proud to serve families across Longmont, Lyons, Hygiene, Berthoud, Niwot, and Gunbarrel with teen orthodontics, adult orthodontics, Invisalign®, Invisalign Teen®, clear aligners, and braces for kids.
